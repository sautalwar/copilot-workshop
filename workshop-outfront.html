Why Guardrails Matter

🔒 Input Validation

Sanitize inputs, reject injection patterns. The AI can be tricked — your server cannot.

⏱ Rate Limiting

Prevent runaway costs. Max 10 requests/minute. Your API bill stays predictable.

📋 Audit Logging

Log every tool call with timestamp, input, output. Know exactly what the AI accessed.

🔍 Response Filtering

Strip sensitive data — internal IDs, PII, credentials never reach the AI model.
